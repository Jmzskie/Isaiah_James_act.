Networking Terms

Internet

The Internet is a global network that connects millions of private, public, academic, business, and government networks.

Intranet

An intranet is a private network within an organization that uses internet technologies to share information, collaborate, and provide various services exclusively to its internal members.

Extranet

An extranet is an extension of an intranet that allows limited access to external users, such as business partners, suppliers, or customers.

LAN (Local Area Network)

A LAN is a network that connects computers and devices within a limited geographical area, such as a home, office, or campus.

WAN (Wide Area Network)

A WAN is a network that spans a larger geographical area, often connecting multiple LANs. It uses various technologies to enable communication over longer distances, such as between cities or countries.

My Favorite Recipe Tinola

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 pc onion, small -sized, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 pc ginger, cut into strips
  • Â½ kilo chicken, cut into 8 pcs
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 pcs Knorr chicken cubes
  • 1 pc chayote or 1 pc small - sized green papaya, sliced
  • 2 stalks moringa leaves

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pot.
  2. When the onion starts to get soft, add the chicken. Cook for 5 minutes or until it turns light brown.
  3. Pour the water. Let boil. Cover and then set the heat to low. Boil for 40 minutes.
  4. Scoop and discard the scums and oil on the soup. Add the Knorr chicken cube and chayote or papaya. Stir.
  5. Cover and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Put the malunggay and hot pepper leaves in the pot and pour the fish sauce in. Continue to cook for 2 minutes.
  7. Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve.
  8. Share and enjoy!
